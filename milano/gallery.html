Small gallery of the most beautiful places in Milan

Duomo di Milano

The city center, church whose construction begins in 1386 and ends in 1965. Fascinating, the attention to detail is enormous, to appreciate accurately its details, it would take days.

Door of Duomo

One of the doors of the Duomo of Milan, realized in bronze, is "only" a door but it is a real work of art that tells historical religious events.

Castello Sforzesco

Castle that has a great history behind it, inside you can visit museums and a huge park behind him in case you want to have a picnic.

Stutue of Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo da Vinci and his students, painter, designer, engineer, scientist, theorist, sculptor and architect. A Genius of the High Renaissance.

Galleria Vittorio Emanuele

Inside we will find luxury restaurants, and big fashion brands such as Versace, Luis Vuitton, Prada. The structure is magnificent, on the ground there is also a bull, where people turn three times in its private parts to have good luck.

Three Towers

Milan is also a modern city, these three towers are the example, each different from the other and with a unique feature, next to there is a commercial center and a large park.
