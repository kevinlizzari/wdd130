Content

Home page

Welcome to Milan! We will discover together some beauties of this modern city, considered the capital of fashion and design around the world. Cities with ancient beauties like the Duomo, the Sforzesco Castle and modern beauties like the Three Towers, and a world of pieces of art around the streets of Milan. We will see a bit together what are the places we can not miss to see if we come to Milan

Images for the Home page

[Gallery]

Small gallery of the most beautiful places in Milan

Duomo di Milano, the city center, church whose construction begins in 1386 and ends in 1965. Fascinating, the attention to detail is enormous, to appreciate accurately its details, it would take days.

One of the doors of the Duomo of Milan, realized in bronze, is "only" a door but it is a real work of art that tells historical religious events

Castello Sforzesco, has a great history behind it, inside you can visit museums and a huge park behind him in case you want to have a picnic

Stutue of Leonardo da Vinci and his students, painter, designer, engineer, scientist, theorist, sculptor and architect. A Genius of the High Renaissance

Galleria Vittorio Emanuele, inside we will find luxury restaurants, and big fashion brands such as Versace, Luis Vuitton, Prada. The structure is magnificent, on the ground there is also a bull, where people turn three times in its private parts to have good luck

Three Towers, Milan is also a modern city, these three towers are the example, each different from the other and with a unique feature, next to there is a commercial center and a large park
